# STEP 2 Results — Refinement Round 2 (Phase 3B: 5 → 3)

This section documents the refinement process from five candidates down to the final three selections based on improvements, usability, visual appeal, and feature completeness.

---

## Candidate Evaluations

### Plan 026
- Added accurate spin history tracking (really creative and a unique feature compared to others) 
- Introduced new animation enhancements  
- Pay table is now scrollable  
- Updated background design  
- Added a dedicated token panel  
- Includes a small win animation (inconsistent trigger noted)  

---

### Plan 004
- Side lever animation behaves unexpectedly (moves right instead of down)  
- Visually overwhelming interface  
- Includes a sequence of light animations  

---

### Plan 034
- Interface dynamically resizes (narrows when lever is pulled; expands at higher values such as 1020+)  
- Strong aesthetic design; visually comfortable and easy on the eyes  

---

### Plan 021
- Remains overly wide in layout  
- Less visually appealing compared to other candidates  
- Lacks polish in overall design  

---

### Plan 047
- Requires purchasing tokens  
- Includes engaging light effects  
- Displays net profit/loss (P/L), providing useful gameplay feedback  

---

## Final Selection

The following plans were selected to move forward after the entire group voted for their top 3 favorites:
- **Plan 034**
- **Plan 026**
- **Plan 047**

---

## Summary
Plans 034, 026, and 047 were chosen due to their stronger balance of visual appeal, interactivity, and feature improvements. These candidates demonstrated clearer progress in refinement, better user feedback systems, and more engaging gameplay elements compared to the others.
