# STEP 3 Results — Refinement Round 3 (Phase 4: 3 → 2)

This section documents the refinement process from three shortlisted candidates down to the final two selections based on usability improvements, feature clarity, interaction design, and overall polish.

---

## Candidate Evaluations

### Plan 034
- Introduced a narrower panel, improving layout balance and reducing unused space  
- Added dual interaction options (lever click or button press), increasing usability and accessibility  
- Supports customizable token betting, enhancing player control  
- Displays clear and concise history without overwhelming the user  
- Maintains a clean interface with minimal unnecessary text  
- Includes win/loss animations that remain consistent and polished  

---

### Plan 026
- Retains strong features such as spin history tracking and animation enhancements  
- Interface improvements from previous round remain intact  
- However, fewer notable refinements were introduced in this phase compared to others  
- Still slightly less intuitive in terms of feature accessibility  

---

### Plan 047
- Introduced statistics tracking, adding depth to gameplay feedback  
- Maintains clear spin history display  
- Visual design remains similar to pre-refinement (limited visual iteration)  
- Continues to provide strong feedback elements such as P/L tracking
